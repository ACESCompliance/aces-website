What does the free assessment include?
We walk your facility (in person for PA locations, or via video for remote), identify OSHA compliance gaps against applicable 1910 standards, and deliver a sample report showing findings, estimated penalty exposure, and recommended corrective actions. No cost. No obligation.
How is this different from hiring a safety consultant?
Most consultants come in once, hand you a binder, and leave. ACES provides ongoing monthly audits with branded reports, corrective action tracking, and a dedicated monthly review call. It's a fractional safety director — not a one-time audit.
What types of warehouses do you serve?
We serve warehousing and distribution operations including 3PLs, fulfillment centers, cold storage, and manufacturing facilities with warehouse components. Currently serving the Pennsylvania I-81 Corridor and Lehigh Valley region.

We audit against all applicable OSHA 1910 General Industry standards including walking-working surfaces, fire protection, electrical, powered industrial trucks, hazard communication, PPE, lockout/tagout, and more. Every finding is cited to the specific CFR code.
